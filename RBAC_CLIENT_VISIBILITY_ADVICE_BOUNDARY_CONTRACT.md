## 1. Executive Decision

**Artefact status:** `RBAC_CLIENT_VISIBILITY_ADVICE_BOUNDARY_CONTRACT_ACCEPTED_WITH_DOWNSTREAM_DEPENDENCIES`

**Safety gate status:** `RBAC_CLIENT_VISIBILITY_ADVICE_BOUNDARY_GATE_CONTRACTED_NOT_IMPLEMENTED`

**Codex readiness status:** `CODEX_HANDOFF_NOT_READY`

This artefact locks the AlphaVest WealthOS core P0 safety contract for RBAC, route/action/object permissions, payload visibility, fail-closed client visibility, AI Draft internal-only handling, the no-unapproved-advice boundary, advisor approval versus compliance release separation, and admin non-bypass. It is a contract artefact only. It does not implement code, change APIs, change Prisma, create tests, generate screens, generate state-screens, create images, create Codex tasks or prepare a final Codex handoff.

The contract preserves the binding decision that AlphaVest is a digital, human-backed, evidence-backed Family Office workflow platform. The MVP may use AI/rules drafting internally, but no unapproved advice, AI Draft, internal rationale, compliance notes, unreleased evidence or unredacted export content may reach a client. Route access is not action permission. Action permission is not payload visibility. Admin power is operational and governance-scoped; it is not a bypass of advice, compliance, evidence, audit, visibility or export gates.
