## Buyer Personas

| Persona | Qualifies when | Excludes when |
|---|---|---|
| Family Office Principal/CFO | Needs evidence-backed decision control across family, entity and advisor workflows. | Seeks autonomous final financial, legal or tax advice. |
| Advisor / operating partner | Owns advisor review, evidence collection and client-safe delivery quality. | Needs production banking, custody or trade execution integrations in the pilot. |
| Compliance / risk owner | Cares about approval separation, auditability, redaction and no-bypass controls. | Requires regulated production deployment certification before design partnership. |
| Trust-led buyer | Buys confidence that internal work cannot leak into client-visible advice. | Wants mass-market self-serve onboarding or GA procurement terms. |

## Pilot Criteria

Good design partner:

- accepts demo-data closed pilot boundaries
- can provide feedback on evidence, review, compliance, client visibility and export gates
- has a named business owner and a named compliance/risk owner
- understands the pilot is human-reviewed and evidence-backed
- values proof that wrong-tenant, admin-bypass and internal-payload leakage fail closed

Exclusion criteria:

- needs GA procurement or production SLA now
- needs autonomous final advice or trade execution
- needs real client data before a later written approval exists
- needs KYC/suitability/committee routes as live V1.0 commitments
- treats screenshots as safety proof

## Demo Narrative

Primary story: AlphaVest is not a dashboard-first demo. It is a trust spine for family-office decisions.

| Order | Process | Demo role | Proof |
|---:|---|---|---|
| 1 | BP-001 | Relationship intake process | Mapped tenant actor reaches first client-safe decision only after evidence, advisor and compliance gates. |
| 2 | BP-024 | Document upload process | Upload and review stay separate from evidence sufficiency and release. |
| 3 | BP-046 | Rebuild-with-evidence process | AI/rules draft and internal rationale remain internal-only. |
| 4 | BP-020 | Admin non-bypass proof | Admin/governance powers cannot force release, export, visibility or sufficiency. |
| 5 | BP-017 | Cross-tenant denial proof | Wrong tenant or wrong object fails closed without payload leakage. |
| 6 | BP-088 | Export trust output | Export package requires scope, redaction, approval and audit. |

Safe talk track:

1. Policy frame: no unapproved advice reaches the client.
2. Evidence frame: uploaded material becomes reviewable evidence, not instant sufficiency.
3. Internal draft frame: analyst/advisor work remains internal until compliance release.
4. Compliance frame: advisor approval is not client release.
5. Client frame: client sees only released, client-safe projection.
6. Export frame: package output is scoped, redacted, approved and audited.

## Advice Boundary And Pricing Frame

Offer: controlled paid design-partner pilot.

Allowed positioning:

- human-reviewed workflow proof
- evidence-backed decision support
- client-safe visibility and export gates
- design-partner feedback loop
- demo-data pilot until written real-data approval exists

Forbidden positioning:

- mass-market GA
- regulated production ready
- autonomous financial/legal/tax advice
- real client data approved
- banking, custody or trade execution integration

Paid pilot terms should be framed around learning value and trust proof, not production compliance certification. Pricing language should stay conditional: pilot fee, limited cohort, defined feedback cadence, no real-data onboarding unless a later approval expands scope.
