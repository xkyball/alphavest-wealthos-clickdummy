## Common troubleshooting patterns

| Pattern | Check | User-facing explanation |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Wrong role | Confirm the top-bar role or demo session. | The current role may not be allowed to view or complete the action. |
| Wrong tenant | Confirm the tenant selector. | The object may belong to another client tenant. |
| Missing evidence | Review evidence checklist and upload/request required item. | Release or decision cannot continue until proof is complete. |
| Compliance hold | Open compliance review/block state. | Advice-like material stays internal until compliance releases it. |
| Export blocked | Check scope, redaction, approval and expiry. | Export packages must be role-safe, redacted and audited. |
| Static/no-op control | Compare UI behavior to UI reality check. | The current demo may show a future action before persistence is implemented. |
